Our Services

Full Roof Replacement

When repairs are no longer cost-effective, a full replacement is the smartest long-term investment you can make in your home. PeakRidge handles every step — from tear-off to final inspection.

What's included:

  • Complete tear-off and disposal of existing materials
  • Deck inspection and repair of any damaged sheathing
  • Ice & water shield along eaves, valleys, and penetrations
  • Synthetic underlayment across the full deck
  • GAF or Owens Corning architectural shingles (your choice)
  • New flashing at all transitions, walls, and chimneys
  • Ridge cap and proper attic ventilation assessment
  • Full magnetic sweep and debris removal

Typical residential replacement is completed in one day. Written warranty on labour plus manufacturer warranty on materials.


Our Services

Repairs & Patching

Not every roof problem requires a full replacement. PeakRidge offers fast, targeted repairs to stop damage in its tracks — often within the same week you call.

Common repairs we handle:

  • Active leaks and interior water intrusion
  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Failed or lifted flashing at chimneys and skylights
  • Storm and hail damage (insurance claims supported)
  • Flat roof membrane splits and blisters
  • Gutter detachment and fascia rot

We provide a written assessment before any work begins so you know exactly what you're paying for.


Our Services

Flat & Low-Slope Roofing

Flat and low-slope roofs demand specialized systems and installers who understand drainage, membrane integrity, and long-term performance. PeakRidge is certified in all major flat roofing systems.

Systems we install:

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) — energy-efficient, heat-welded seams, excellent for commercial applications
  • EPDM (Rubber Roofing) — durable, low-maintenance, ideal for residential flat roofs
  • Modified Bitumen — multi-layer system offering superior puncture and weather resistance

All flat roof installations include a full drainage review and internal drain or scupper assessment.


Our Services

Gutters & Drainage

A properly functioning gutter system protects your foundation, siding, and landscaping from water damage. PeakRidge installs and services seamless aluminum gutters across the GTA.

Our gutter services:

  • Seamless aluminum gutters (custom-formed on site)
  • Leaf guards and mesh gutter protection systems
  • Downspout extensions and underground drainage connections
  • Fascia board replacement and gutter re-hang
  • Spring and fall gutter cleaning packages

Available in standard white, brown, black, and custom colour-matched to your home's trim.
